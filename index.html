The Heart of the Olds

Sighisoara considered the largest medieval citadel n Europe, that is to this day densely populated. Being the magical medieval heart of Transylvania, it offers many attractions and festivities that lure in many visitors. Such attractions are: The Clock Tower, The Scholar's Stairs, The Blacksmith's Tower and many more. The city holds many secrets and is always ready to be explored by the brave visitors who dare to come.

Dragons descend yearly

The most popular festival is in the height of summer, at the end of July, the medieval festival organized yearly by the dragon knights. The organizers gather artists from across the country to create a perfect, magical and joy sparking experience with the essence of old and medieval for their citizens and wandering visitors to enjoy for three days and three nights.
